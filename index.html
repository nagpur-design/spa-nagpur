Welcome to Nagpur Spa

Our Services

💆‍♀️ Massage Therapy

Our expert massage therapists will help you relax and rejuvenate with our range of massage therapies, including Swedish, deep tissue, and hot stone massages.

🌿 Aromatherapy

Our aromatherapy sessions use essential oils to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and improve overall well-being. Choose from a range of scents, including lavender, chamomile, and eucalyptus.

💅 Facial Treatments

Our facial treatments include a range of options, from basic cleansing and moisturizing to advanced treatments like chemical peels and microdermabrasion.

👣 Reflexology

Our reflexology sessions target specific pressure points on the feet to promote relaxation, reduce pain, and improve overall health.

🏋️‍♀️ Body Wraps

Our body wraps use natural ingredients like seaweed, mud, and herbs to detoxify and nourish the skin, leaving you feeling smooth and refreshed.

👫 Couples Spa Packages

Our couples spa packages include a range of treatments, from massages and facials to body wraps and aromatherapy, all designed to help you and your partner relax and rejuvenate together.
